Rock Paper Scissors

How to play

  • Press the 'rock', 'paper' or 'scissors' button below
  • The computer will select their choice
  • Rock beats scissors
  • Paper beats rock
  • Scissors beats paper
  • If you win, your score will increase
  • If the computer wins, their score will increase
  • Press the reset button to reset both scores back to 0
  • Press "Play again", if you want to play another round against the computer
  • Press 'How to play' to remove these instructions and start playing
