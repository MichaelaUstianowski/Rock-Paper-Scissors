Rock Paper Scissors

How to play

  • Select your choice: rock, paper or scissors
  • The computer will select their choice
  • Rock beats scissors
  • Paper beats rock
  • Scissors beats paper
  • If you win, your score will increase. If the computer wins, their score will increase
  • Press the reset button to reset both scores back to 0
